Key Indian sports scholarships & support

Not a complete list for the whole country, but a starter pack of schemes students from places like Maharashtra / Pune commonly hear about.

Khelo India Athlete Scholarship

Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports / Sports Authority of India

Level: Elite youth (national) Type: Govt of India
Typical support: training + support worth ~₹6.28 lakh/year, including ~₹1.2 lakh yearly out-of-pocket allowance (as per latest scheme documents).

Who it's for: Khelo India Athletes selected from national-level competitions and talent identification camps. Once selected, athletes train at recognised academies with hostel, coaching, competition exposure and a monthly allowance paid directly to their bank account.

Why it matters for our site: students from rural or small-town backgrounds who perform well can basically get full training + stay covered instead of paying themselves.

Good for: serious medal prospects Check: kheloindia.gov.in / MYAS notices Search "Khelo India Athlete Scholarship latest notice"
Maharashtra Sports Scholarship (School Games)

Department of Sports & Youth Services, Govt of Maharashtra

Level: School (till 12th) Type: State Govt
Typical support: annual scholarship amounts from a few thousand rupees upwards based on place (1st/2nd/3rd/participation) in school/national-level competitions.

Who it's for: Students who secure positions at School Games, National School Games and sometimes international school-level competitions. Amounts differ by medal and level but are paid directly as a scholarship for that year.

Why it matters for our site: many Maharashtra students don't know that school medals can convert to yearly scholarship money, which can help with books, coaching or hostel costs.

Good for: strong school players Check: mahasports.maharashtra.gov.in (scholarship section) Search "Maharashtra school sports scholarship latest GR"
State Cash Awards for Khelo India Medalists

Govt of Maharashtra (for medal winners representing state)

Level: School / Youth Type: State Govt
Typical support: one-time cash awards in lakh range for gold/silver/bronze at Khelo India youth events (amounts depend on year and state policy).

Who it's for: Athletes who win medals at Khelo India Youth / University Games while representing Maharashtra. Separate from central Khelo India athlete support, this is a state-level reward.

Why it matters for our site: shows students that big performances can create real prize money, which families often reinvest into equipment, coaching or travel.

Good for: medal targets Check: state GRs, sports department news Search "Maharashtra cash award Khelo India medalist"
ONGC Sports Scholarship Scheme

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. (ONGC)

Level: Mostly 15–20 yrs Type: PSU / Corporate
Typical support: monthly stipend for selected players, different slabs for national/international performers (exact amount as per that year's notification).

Who it's for: Young athletes with strong national performance who need financial help to continue training. Applications are usually online, with a list of supported sports and age limits.

Why it matters for our site: many college-going athletes from places like Pune dream of corporate backing; this scheme is a classic example of PSU support.

Good for: national medalists in popular sports Check: sportsscholarship.ongc.co.in Search "ONGC Sports Scholarship 2025-26 notification"
IndianOil "Acers" Sports Scholarship

IndianOil Corporation – IndianOil Sports Scholarship (Acers)

Level: School / College Type: PSU / Corporate
Typical support: fixed yearly scholarship amount plus support for training/competition as per official scheme.

Who it's for: promising young athletes in selected sports, usually with good national or state performance. Helps with kit, travel and training expenses.

Why it matters for our site: shows how PSUs adopt players and build long-term relationship, sometimes even leading to jobs later.

Good for: consistent performers Check: IndianOil official sports scholarship page Search "IndianOil sports scholarship Acers"
BPCL Sports Scholarship

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL)

Level: roughly 13–25 yrs Type: PSU / Corporate
Typical support: monthly stipend (different for budding / junior / elite categories), plus kit and tournament expense support.

Who it's for: athletes placed into categories like Budding, Junior and Elite based on performance. Encourages them to keep training seriously without dropping out due to money issues.

Why it matters for our site: good example of how a company can layer support based on how far the athlete has already reached.

Good for: rising national/probable players Check: BPCL sports scholarship announcements Search "BPCL Sports Scholarship official notification"
University & Private Sports Scholarships

Universities / Colleges (examples: LPU and others)

Level: College / UG / PG Type: Private / University
Typical support: tuition fee concessions, hostel discounts, free access to gym/indoor facilities; exact money value depends on university rules and sports category.

Who it's for: students admitted under sports quota or with strong sports CV. Many universities fix limited seats and offer different levels of fee waiver based on the level of achievement.

Why it matters for our site: students in cities like Pune often apply to multiple colleges. Knowing that sports can reduce fees makes continuing sport more realistic for families.

Good for: balancing studies + sport Check: each college's sports quota prospectus Search "sports quota scholarship + college name"
